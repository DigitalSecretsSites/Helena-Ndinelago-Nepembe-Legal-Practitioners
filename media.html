Recent Changes to Namibian Property Law

By Helena Ndinelago Nepembe | 5 min read

This comprehensive article examines the recent amendments to Namibia's property laws and their implications for buyers, sellers, and developers. The changes, which came into effect earlier this year, represent the most significant overhaul of property legislation in over a decade.

Key topics covered include:

  • New disclosure requirements for property sellers
  • Changes to the transfer duty calculation
  • Updated regulations for sectional title developments
  • Implications for foreign property buyers
