Helena Ndinelago Nepembe

Founder & Principal Legal Practitioner

Helena Ndinelago Nepembe is the founder and principal legal practitioner of the firm, bringing over 20 years of legal experience to her practice. She specializes in property law and civil litigation, with a proven track record of successful outcomes for her clients.

After graduating with honors from the University of Namibia, Helena began her career at one of Windhoek's leading corporate law firms before establishing her own practice to provide more personalized legal services.

Helena is recognized for her meticulous approach to legal matters and her ability to explain complex legal concepts in clear, understandable terms. She is deeply committed to her clients' success and takes pride in building long-term relationships based on trust and results.

Education

  • Admitted as Legal Practitioner of the High Court of Namibia, 2000
  • Advanced Diploma in Property Law, 2005
